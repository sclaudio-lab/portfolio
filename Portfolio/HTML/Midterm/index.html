Christian Claudio has over ten years experience in animation and game art. He has worked as a 3D modeler and concept artist several prominent gaming industries such as Blizzard and NCSoft. Claudio’s artwork is currently on display at the Elena Fine Art Gallery in Laguna Beach, California. Claudio’s online portfolio includes many of the same pieces that are available at the gallery. As an artist, Claudio states that he, “would love to make art that other people can connect with and be inspired by.” Please enjoy the following portfolio of his greatest works.
